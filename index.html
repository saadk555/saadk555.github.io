Fin-Alytics AI

FinTech // AI-Powered Analytics

The Challenge: Building a real-time data processing engine for investment portfolio analysis that could handle millions of data points with minimal latency.

My Role: As Lead Software Engineer & Technical Co-founder, I architected the core Python backend, implemented a scalable DevOps pipeline on AWS, and was instrumental in early-stage client acquisition and technical sales demos.

Tech Stack: Python, Django, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS (EC2, S3, RDS), PostgreSQL, Redis.

The Outcome: Successfully processed 10M+ data points daily, leading to a 30% increase in analytical efficiency for our pilot clients and securing our pre-seed funding round of $500k.

ConnectSphere

DevOps // Scalable Cloud Solutions

The Challenge: A rapidly growing B2B SaaS company faced inconsistent deployments and frequent downtime, hindering their ability to scale and retain enterprise clients.

My Role: I was brought on as a DevOps consultant to design and implement a robust CI/CD and infrastructure-as-code strategy. My work involved creating automated pipelines and deploying monitoring solutions.

Tech Stack: Terraform, Ansible, Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes, Google Cloud Platform (GKE, Cloud Build), Prometheus, Grafana.

The Outcome: Reduced manual deployment time from 4 hours to 15 minutes. Increased system uptime from 99.5% to 99.99%. This stability was a key factor in the company securing a $2M Series A funding round.
